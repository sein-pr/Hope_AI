Educational Topics

What You'll Learn

What is Uranium?

Uranium is a heavy metal used as fuel in nuclear reactors. It occurs naturally and is mined from the earth. Learn about its formation, isotopes, and how it powers clean energy.

Mining Methods: ISR vs Open Pit

In-Situ Recovery (ISR) is a less invasive technique compared to traditional open-pit mining. Explore the pros, cons, and environmental impacts of each method.

Namibia’s Role in Global Uranium Supply

Namibia is one of the top producers of uranium worldwide. Discover the country’s major mines, export routes, and its strategic position in the clean energy future.

Environmental Impact

Mining and radiation raise valid concerns. Understand how regulations, IAEA standards, and technologies are minimizing the ecological footprint of uranium mining.
